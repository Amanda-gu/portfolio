Amanda Guo is a multidisciplinary designer working across brand, interactive web, motion, print, and type. She is currently based in New York. She is always experimenting, iterating, and pushing the boundaries of what is possible. She is also a coffee addict, a cat lover, and a traveller.

She is currently working as a freelance designer, and has worked with clients such as Google, UCLA, and Kemper Museum of Contemporary Art. Previously, she has worked at Use All Five and Marian Goodman Gallery. She is open to work and collaborations.
